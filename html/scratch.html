Experience

Karen Cole is a gifted recording artist and experienced music instructor who has dedicated her life to sharing the joy of music. With over eight years of formal training in both piano and voice, Karen has honed her craft and developed a deep understanding of music theory, technique, and performance. Her rich background includes collaborating with the inspirational music platform Strive to Be, showcasing her talents at Broadway Loft Concerts, and writing original music that resonates with audiences. These experiences have made her a versatile and accomplished musician, perfectly suited to teach aspiring singers and pianists of all levels.
